## Current Status as of April 2026

- **Role 1**: Executive Director, Finishing the Task (FTT) coalition — global Great Commission coordination
- **Role 2**: Honorary Chancellor, Spurgeon's College, London — presides at July graduations
- **Role 3**: Founding Pastor (emeritus), Saddleback Church, Lake Forest, CA
- **Active production**: Daily Hope devotional radio/podcast; active on X (@RickWarren) and Facebook
- **Health**: Spinal myoclonus (tremors, blurred vision) — disclosed 2021, worsening
- **Primary focus**: "Gospel for Everyone by 2033" — mobilizing global evangelical cooperation for the 2000th anniversary of the Great Commission
- **Political posture**: Attempting to remain above partisan politics; facing increasing difficulty as American evangelicalism polarizes
